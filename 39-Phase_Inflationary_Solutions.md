We construct the class of inflationary solutions in Φ-Wave Theory, showing that accelerated cosmological expansion arises naturally from metastable or slowly evolving phase configurations. This establishes inflation not as an external scalar-field add-on, but as a natural dynamical regime of the fundamental phase field.

\frac{\ddot a}{a}
=
-\frac{4\pi G}{3}(\rho + 3P)
